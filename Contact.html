Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes, we offer delivery across London within a 10-mile radius of our bakery. Delivery fees vary based on distance, and same-day delivery is available for orders placed before 12 PM.

How far in advance should I order a custom cake?

We recommend ordering custom cakes at least 48 hours in advance. For elaborate wedding cakes or large orders, please contact us at least 2 weeks ahead to ensure availability.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! We offer gluten-free, dairy-free, vegan, and nut-free options. Please specify your dietary requirements when placing your order, and we'll ensure your treats are prepared accordingly.

Can I schedule a bakery tour or tasting?

Yes, we offer bakery tours and tasting sessions for weddings and large events. Please contact us at least one week in advance to schedule an appointment.
