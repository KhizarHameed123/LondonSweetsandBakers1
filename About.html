The SweetBakers Journey

From a small family bakery to London's favorite sweet destination

Our Humble Beginnings

Founded in 1985 by Margaret and James Henderson, SweetBakers started as a small neighborhood bakery in London. With just one oven and a dream, they began crafting traditional British pastries and breads that quickly won the hearts of the local community.

Margaret's secret family recipes, passed down through three generations, became the foundation of our signature treats. James' passion for artisan bread-making brought a new dimension to our offerings, creating a perfect blend of tradition and innovation.

Today, while we've grown to multiple locations across London, we still bake with the same love and attention to detail that Margaret and James started with almost four decades ago.

Our Mission & Philosophy

At SweetBakers, our mission is simple: to bring joy to our customers through quality, creativity, and exceptional service. We believe that every bite should tell a story of craftsmanship and passion.

We source our ingredients from trusted local suppliers who share our commitment to quality. From organic flour to farm-fresh eggs and seasonal fruits, every component is carefully selected to ensure the finest results.

Our philosophy blends time-honored baking techniques with modern innovations. We respect tradition while embracing creativity, resulting in products that are both familiar and excitingly new.

Our Journey Through Time

Milestones that shaped our story

1985

The Beginning

Margaret and James Henderson open the first SweetBakers shop on Baker Street with a single oven and big dreams.

1995

First Expansion

We open our second location in Covent Garden, introducing our famous artisan bread selection to a wider audience.

2005

20th Anniversary

Celebrating two decades of baking excellence, we launch our premium cake collection that becomes an instant hit.

2015

Third Generation

Emily Henderson joins the family business, bringing modern baking techniques while preserving traditional recipes.

2023

Modernization

We launch our online store and nationwide delivery, making our products accessible to sweet lovers across the UK.
